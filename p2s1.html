QuadCopter

This is the Embedded Capstone for my Senior year. The whole project took about one month to complete. This was by far the most intensive class for me and our group spent countless of hours on it. The whole project mainly includes 4 cards, sensors card, flight controller, power card and rasberry pie for User interface and Video streaming. I was responsile to work on the I2C and the Sensors card(pic18F22k25) with 3axis accelerometer, 3 axis gyroscope and the 3 axis compass(IMU). Also, I worked on the video streaming of the rasberry pie and the web interface for viewing the Video Stream. The experience that I got from the class was mainly self-learning and collaboration with my partner.
