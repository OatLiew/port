One Ride Away



This is the poster presentation of our project, One Ride Away, a carpooling mobile application software that our group came up with. The poster presentation gave me a valuable learning experience in design, something I do not often get from engineering classes. Our group tried to come up with the simplest design possible in order for the reader to follow through easily. In order to do this, we tried to balance out the words and the graphic in the poster. Also, we used the idea of white space to emphasize the significant part of the poster. We also came up with the business plan and the user interface design of the mobile. It was a valuable experience in trying to form and come up with the start-up companies.
